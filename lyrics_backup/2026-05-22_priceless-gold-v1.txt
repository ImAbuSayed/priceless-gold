[Hook 1]
Kaaliyan raatan vich main takda haan tera glow
Naina de ishareyan ch labhda haan magic show
Mera dil dhadakda ae kalli vekh ke shining light
Tainu main apna banaunda haan in the night

[Hook 2]
Jadon tu nede hasdi ae ruk janda ae time
Main sab bhul janda haan vekh ke tera crime
Tere nakhre nu jarda haan standing tall
Ishq ch dubbda haan baby giving all

[Verse 1]
Bheed de vich vi tu kalli disdi ae so bright
Main nukkran ch shant khada haan holding tight
Tere husn da wadda nasha karda haan in my mind
Main tere piche aunda haan leaving all behind
Dil de zakham mere lukk gaye ne with no tears
Main ishq ch agge turda haan facing fears
Kanch de gilaas vich dubbdi ae meri memory
Main tainu hi chaunda haan making history

[Pre-Chorus]
Hawaavan vich mithi ghul gayi ae teri scent

Main tere layi ikk jeonda haan begging to stay

[Chorus]
Main agg wangu nitt balda haan feeling heat
Tere piche bhajj aunda haan missing beat

Main poori karda haan teri har desire

[Verse 2]
